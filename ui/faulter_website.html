The platform

Three things FAULTER does

A layered AI system that monitors, visualizes, and alerts — before a fault becomes a stoppage.

01 — Detect

Anomaly engine

A multi-layer AI agent pipeline continuously validates physics signals — velocity, tension, torque, temperature — and scores each subsystem for drift in real time.

02 — Visualize

Live 3D digital twin

Every component rendered as a live wireframe model. Fault scores propagate to geometry — affected parts illuminate in the fault's severity color as anomalies develop.

03 — Act

Graded alerts

Warning, fault, and critical — each with a full audit trail of which signals escalated and when. Operators see it before the line sees it.
